Step 7 Prototype — Build & Test Your Chair

A prototype is a physical or scaled model of your design. It doesn't need to be perfect — it just needs to be good enough to test your key ideas. This is where your thinking becomes real. Plan your build steps before you start — think before you cut! Sketch or photograph your finished prototype below. Test your design against your specification and gather feedback.

💡 Tip Cardboard, tape, foam, wire, and string can go a very long way! Focus on building the most important features first — the ones that solve your core problem.
